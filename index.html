About Me

My passions for psychology, social justice, diversity, and inclusion have led me to pursue a major in Psychology, and a minor in Disability and Inclusion. I also contribute to academic journals, and e-courses which empower neurodivergent individuals, and touch on intersectionality. I want to continue to both academically, and professionally develop in these areas.

Too often in this world, especially when marginalized, our worth is demarcated by social, environmental and political limits. My professional dream is to be a part of abolishing and deconstructing these systems, to ultimately empower those often disenfranchised. Through both lived experience and creative means, I will continue to do my part in making this an ongoing reality.

The sky is not the limit, we can, and have reached further than that!
